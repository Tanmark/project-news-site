Stockhom the Capital of Scandinavia

With over 700 miles of bike lanes, Stockholm is perfect for cycling. There are bike paths and bike lanes all over and you can easily get around between the areas of inner-city Stockholm and out on Djurgården, an area of outstanding natural beauty.

Many locals opt for a bike when commuting to work, or just getting around. But if you're visiting you've probably left your bike back home.

Luckily there are plenty of bike rental shops spread across town, great either for anyone who wants a relaxing afternoon trip or planning a cycling weekend on the islands of the Stockholm archipelago.
